Research

My research lies in Lie theory and the representation theory of reductive groups. My doctoral work studies the geometric relationship between the representations of p-adic groups and real reductive groups, in the spirit of the Adams–Barbasch–Vogan framework and the local Langlands correspondence.

I am advised by Prof. Leticia Barchini at the Department of Mathematics, Oklahoma State University.
